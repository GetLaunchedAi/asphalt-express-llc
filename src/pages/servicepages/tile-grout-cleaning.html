Tile & Grout Cleaning FAQs

Answers to common questions about our tile and grout cleaning services by Miracle Carpet Care.

  • We clean all types of tile including ceramic, porcelain, natural stone, marble, granite, and travertine. Our process is safe for all tile types and we adjust our cleaning methods accordingly.

  • We recommend professional tile and grout cleaning every 12-18 months for most households. High-traffic areas or homes with pets may require more frequent cleaning to maintain optimal appearance and hygiene.

  • Grout sealing creates a protective barrier that repels dirt and stains, making future cleaning easier. It's especially recommended for light-colored grout or high-traffic areas.

  • Most tile and grout cleaning jobs take 2-4 hours depending on the size of the area and condition of the grout. We work efficiently while ensuring thorough cleaning and proper drying time.

  • We stand behind every job 100% with our iron-clad risk-free guarantee. If you aren't happy with our work, we'll reclean the area for free. And if you still aren't pleased, you pay nothing.
