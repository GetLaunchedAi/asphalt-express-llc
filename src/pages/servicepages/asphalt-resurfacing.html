FAQ's

Asphalt Resurfacing FAQs

Answers to common questions about our asphalt resurfacing services by Asphalt Express LLC.

  • Resurfacing is ideal when the base is still in good condition but the surface is worn or damaged. It's more cost-effective than complete replacement when the foundation is solid.

  • Typically 1-2 days depending on the size of the project. We work efficiently to minimize disruption while ensuring quality results.

  • Resurfacing involves installing new asphalt over the existing base, while replacement involves removing everything and starting fresh. Resurfacing is more cost-effective when the base is sound.

  • Resurfaced asphalt can last 10-15 years or more with proper maintenance. Regular sealcoating and maintenance will extend the life of your resurfaced surface.

  • Most surfaces can be resurfaced if the base is in good condition. We evaluate each project to determine if resurfacing is the best solution for your needs.
