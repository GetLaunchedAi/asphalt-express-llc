About Us

A Family Business Built on Hard Work and Honesty

At Miracle Carpet Care, we are a family-owned company (father and sons) built for over 35 years on hard work and honesty, knowing that the customer is the backbone of our business. We opened up shop in 1989, and since then we've provided our community with exceptional cleaning services.

We have short turnaround times when we make appointments for our customers. Sometimes we can even do same-day appointments. We try to be on time for our appointments even with the traffic messes we encounter every day! Our customer is the backbone of our business.

"Miracle Carpet Care won an Angie's List Superior Service Award four years in a row, from 2014 to 2017. We stand behind every job 100% with our iron-clad risk-free guarantee. If you aren't happy with our work, we'll reclean the area for free. And if you still aren't pleased, you pay nothing."

Sam Owner & Founder
